Félix has earned the Telecommunication Engineer degree from the University of Sevilla (Spain) and the Master of Science in Computer Engineering from the Polytechnic University of Milano (Italy) thanks to a Double Degree agreement within the T.I.M.E program.

He has developed his professional career in the software domain, starting as a backend developer and progressively expanding his scope into areas such as software analysis, continuous integration, and infrastructure automation. Throughout his career, he has worked in different fields (IoT, telecommunications) and has taken on team management and people lead responsibilities.

Over time, he transitioned from software development to a DevOps and Infrastructure role, with a strong focus on Platform Engineering. He works at the intersection of development and infrastructure, specializing in CI/CD, GitOps, and Developer Experience. His mission is to empower development teams by providing tools and solutions that streamline workflows, enhance automation, and improve efficiency, enabling developers to focus on delivering high-quality software with minimal friction.
